1. BIOGRAPHY (經歷)
================================================================================

Full Name: 羊宮 妃那 (ようみや ひな, Yōmiya Hina)
Born: March 26, 2000 (age 26) — Nara Prefecture, Japan
Agency: 青二製作 (Aoni Production) — Junior → 正所属 (regular member) as of April 11, 2025

Timeline:
- 3rd year of middle school: Inspired by Natsu's lines in "Fairy Tail" to pursue voice acting.
  Started watching anime to keep up with friends, then became obsessed.
- Dec 8, 2019: Passed "Project ANIMA" audition for the SAKUGAN heroine role,
  becoming one of 7 finalists.
- April 11, 2020: Joined Aoni Production (Junior).
- April 9, 2023: Officially confirmed as Takamatsu Tomori, vocalist of MyGO!!!!!
  in BanG Dream!.
- 2024: Won the 18th Seiyu Awards — Best New Actor Award (新人声優賞).
- April 11, 2025: Promoted to regular (正所属) status at Aoni Production.
